About me

Hello! I’m Jerwin Nico Quijano, an aspiring web developer from Lucena City with a passion for bringing creative ideas to life through code. I’m currently focused on front-end development, specializing in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like Flask. My interest in web development began with a desire to understand how websites are built, and since then, I’ve been dedicated to mastering the skills needed to create responsive, user-friendly websites.

As I continue to learn and grow, I’m exploring more advanced topics such as web accessibility, performance optimization, and interactive design. I love diving into new technologies and frameworks, constantly challenging myself to build more efficient and visually engaging web solutions. One of my current projects is a portfolio website built with Flask, where I showcase my latest work and skill

Outside of coding, I enjoy experimenting with design tools like Figma, working on UI/UX concepts, and understanding how aesthetics impact user experience. My goal is to merge clean, modern design with functional code to deliver websites that not only look good but perform well.

I’m always open to learning from others, collaborating on exciting projects, or just connecting with like-minded developers. Feel free to explore my projects and get in touch if you’d like to collaborate or have any opportunities in web development!
